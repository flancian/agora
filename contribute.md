The [[Agora]] is a [[commons]], which means it belongs to everyone who tends it. There are many ways to contribute.

## 1. Grow the Graph (Add Content)
The most direct way to contribute is to add your own [[digital garden]].
*   **See:** [[Join]] and [[signup]].
*   **Action:** Send a pull request or an email to register your repository. Once added, your notes become part of the Agora.

## 2. Tend the Code (Development)
We are open source! Help us build the platform.
*   **Core:** [[agora]] (Configuration & Documentation)
*   **Server:** [[agora-server]] (The Python/Flask app)
*   **Bridge:** [[agora-bridge]] (The data fetcher)
*   **Repo:** https://github.com/flancian/agora

## 3. Sustain the Project
*   **Spread the Word:** Share links to nodes you find interesting.
*   **Financial:** See [[flancia social impact]] or [[donate]] (if available) to support server costs.

## 4. Community Gardening
*   **Fix Links:** If you see a broken link, let the author know or fix it if you can.
*   **Welcome Newcomers:** If you see a new [[person]] in the [[latest]] feed, say hello!
