The [[Agora]] is not just a static wiki; it aspires to be a living, executable environment.

## Actions
We are experimenting with **[[Agora Actions]]**.
*   These are structured blocks in your notes that the Agora can "execute."
*   *Example:* A block might define a recurring reminder, a social signal, or a data transformation.

## Active Nodes
Some nodes are "active" agents.
*   **Bots:** [[agora bot]] can interact with you on Mastodon or Matrix.
*   **Bridge:** The [[agora bridge]] actively fetches and updates content.
